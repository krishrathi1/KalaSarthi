# AI Design Generator - User Guide

## Quick Start Guide for Artisans

### Step 1: Access the Feature

**From Profile Page:**
1. Log in to your artisan account
2. Go to your Profile page
3. Click the "AI Design Generator" button at the top

**From Dashboard:**
1. Log in to your artisan account
2. On the main dashboard, find the "AI Design Generator" card
3. Click "Open" to launch the feature

### Step 2: Select Your Product

1. You'll see a grid of your published products
2. Only products with images are shown
3. Click on any product to select it
4. The selected product will be highlighted with a blue border

**Note:** If you don't see any products:
- Make sure you have published products
- Ensure your products have at least one image
- Go to "Smart Product Creator" to create products first

### Step 3: Choose an Artistic Style

Select one of the 6 available styles:

| Style | Description | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Traditional** | Classic Indian handicraft patterns | Sarees, traditional jewelry, ethnic wear |
| **Modern** | Contemporary minimalist design | Modern accessories, urban fashion |
| **Vibrant** | Bold and energetic patterns | Festival items, decorative pieces |
| **Elegant** | Sophisticated and refined | Premium products, formal wear |
| **Rustic** | Natural and earthy textures | Handmade pottery, wooden crafts |
| **Festive** | Celebratory decorations | Wedding items, festival decorations |

### Step 4: Select Colors

1. Choose up to 6 colors from the color palette
2. Click on a color to select it (border turns blue)
3. Click again to deselect
4. A counter shows how many colors you've selected (X/6)

### Step 5: Generate Variations

1. Click the "Generate Design Variations" button
2. Wait while AI creates your variations (10-30 seconds)
3. A loading spinner shows progress
4. Don't close the page during generation

**What's Happening:**
- AI analyzes your product image
- Applies selected colors while maintaining design
- Generates professional-quality variations
- Preserves product structure and details

### Step 6: View and Download Results

1. Generated variations appear in a grid on the right
2. Each variation shows:
   - The new colored design
   - Color name
   - Download button

3. To download a variation:
   - Click the "Download" button under the image
   - Image saves as: `{ProductName}-{Color}-variation.png`

### Step 7: Use Your Variations

**Ways to use generated variations:**

1. **Product Listings**
   - Add to your product gallery
   - Show customers color options
   - Increase product appeal

2. **Customer Communication**
   - Send to customers asking about colors
   - Show in WhatsApp/email conversations
   - Help customers make decisions

3. **Social Media**
   - Post on Instagram/Facebook
   - Show your design capabilities
   - Attract more customers

4. **Production Planning**
   - Decide which colors to produce
   - Test market interest
   - Plan inventory

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: "No products found"
**Solution:**
1. Go to Smart Product Creator
2. Create and publish products
3. Ensure products have images
4. Return to AI Design Generator

### Problem: Generation takes too long
**Solution:**
1. Reduce number of colors selected
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try again during off-peak hours
4. Contact support if issue persists

### Problem: Poor quality results
**Solution:**
1. Use higher quality source images
2. Try different style options
3. Select more appropriate colors
4. Ensure original image is clear

### Problem: Can't download variations
**Solution:**
1. Check browser download settings
2. Allow downloads from the site
3. Try right-click > Save Image
4. Check available storage space

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How many variations can I generate?
**A:** You can generate up to 6 color variations per request. You can run multiple requests for the same product.

### Q: How long does generation take?
**A:** Typically 10-30 seconds depending on the number of colors selected and server load.

### Q: Can I save variations to my product?
**A:** Currently, you need to download and manually add them. Auto-save feature coming soon!

### Q: Do variations cost money?
**A:** The feature uses your platform subscription. No additional charges per generation.

### Q: Can I use custom colors?
**A:** Currently limited to 10 preset colors. Custom color input coming in future update.

### Q: Will this work on mobile?
**A:** Yes! The interface is fully responsive and works on mobile devices.

### Q: Can I generate for multiple products at once?
**A:** Currently one product at a time. Batch generation coming soon!

### Q: What if I don't like the results?
**A:** Try different styles or colors. You can generate unlimited variations.

## Best Practices

### For Sarees
- Use Traditional or Elegant styles
- Try Red, Blue, Green, Purple
- Show border and pallu clearly

- Use Elegant or Festive styles

### For Pottery
- Use Rustic or Traditional styles
- Try earthy tones (Brown, Orange)
- Show texture clearly
- Use natural lighting

### For Textiles
- Use Vibrant or Modern styles
- Try bold colors (Red, Blue, Purple)
- Show pattern details
- Use flat, even lighting
