# **App Name**: KalaMitra

## Core Features:

- AI Digital Twin: AI Digital Twin: A multilingual AI agent that represents the artisan, answering buyer queries and scaling reach using Gemini 1.5 Flash.
- Heritage Storytelling: Heritage Storytelling: Use a multimodal AI tool that auto-generates product stories, captions, and reels in multiple languages from artisan photos/videos.
- AI-Powered Storefront: Marketplace-as-a-Service (MaaS): Artisans create AI-powered storefronts with auto-generated catalogs and promotional content, served by Nextjs.
- Buyer-Artisan Match: Buyer-Artisan Matchmaking: Connect artisans with buyers using embeddings based on the cultural relevance of the goods being traded.
- Offline-First Mode: Offline-First Digital Mela Mode: Application can function offline storing AI outputs and artisan details locally, and syncing with Google Cloud (Gemini, Firebase) when internet is available. Leverages PWA and local storage.
- Trend Mapper: Cultural Trend Mapper: Suggest modern variations of traditional crafts by analyzing fashion/home d√©cor trends, and incorporating these new trends in marketing with the AI tool.
- Multi-Marketplace Bridge: Multi-Marketplace Auto-Bridge: Auto-list artisan products across multiple marketplaces (Amazon Karigar, Flipkart Samarth, Etsy, Meesho).

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A warm, earthy terracotta (#E07A5F) to reflect the natural materials and craftsmanship of the artisans. The warmth connects to feelings of history, while being very current in design trends.
- Background color: A light, desaturated beige (#F2E8D5), complementing the terracotta, creating a clean and inviting space for content to stand out.
- Accent color: A muted mustard yellow (#D8C3A3) provides a contrasting highlight for key UI elements, echoing traditional textile dyes.
- Body text: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) offers a balance of modern readability with a touch of humanist warmth, ideal for product descriptions and artisan stories.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif), a transitional serif similar to Didot, lends an elegant and high-end feel that's suited to headlines or small amounts of text. Since longer text is anticipated in other areas of the site, 'PT Sans' is a better match for the body text.
- Use hand-drawn style icons with rounded edges to add a personal, authentic touch that reflects the handmade nature of the crafts.
- Employ a grid-based layout with generous spacing and a focus on visual hierarchy to present products and stories in an organized, appealing manner. Focus on a mobile-first design approach.
